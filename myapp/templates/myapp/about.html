About Capricorn District Municipality

ABOUT CAPRICORN DISTRICT MUNICIPALITY

Capricorn District Municipality (CDM) is a Category C municipality located in the heart of Limpopo Province, South Africa. It serves as the administrative hub for three local municipalities: Blouberg, Molemole, and Lepelle-Nkumpi.

🚰 THE WATER CRISIS IN CDM
💧

Failed Water Projects

R213 MILLION spent on bulk water projects in Blouberg with ZERO water delivered after two years. Communities left without promised infrastructure.

🔧

Contractor Negligence

95% of infrastructure breakdowns are ignored by maintenance contractors, leaving communities with unresolved water failures for months.

💰

Budget Mismanagement

R24 MILLION operations and maintenance budget exhausted within the first 3 months of the financial year, leaving no funds for repairs.

🏘️

Silvermine Village Crisis

24+ MONTHS since the residents of Silvermine village last had access to piped clean water. Residents are forced to share water sources with animals.

📱 AQUAREPORT CDM — THE SOLUTION
📝

Easy Fault Reporting

Residents can report water faults instantly through a simple, accessible web form — no app installation required.

📊

Real-Time Tracking

Every report is tracked with status updates: Pending → In Progress → Resolved. Full transparency and accountability.

👥

Community Voices

Each report creates a community persona, putting real faces to the water crisis and amplifying resident voices.

📱🌍

Accessible to All

Works on any device with a browser — Android, iOS, desktop — unlike existing apps that are iOS-only.
