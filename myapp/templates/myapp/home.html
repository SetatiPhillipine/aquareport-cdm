Official Opening: Avon Water Project — Blouberg
Executive Mayor Cllr Mamedupi Teffo invites the community to the opening of the Avon Water Project, enhancing water supply to Ga-Seakamela and surrounding areas. The R22.9 million project includes two newly drilled boreholes, four refurbished boreholes, five pump houses, and 100 standpipes delivering clean water to the community.
📍 Ditlou Sports Ground, Avon, Blouberg Municipality 📅 Sunday, 03 May 2026 | 11h00 📞 Jabu Masondo: 082 302 0566
